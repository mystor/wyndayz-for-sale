2001 Macgregor 26X - "Wyndayz"

We are the second owners of this 2001 motor-sailboat. We bought it in 2006 when our children were young and have enjoyed it for the past 8 years. The kids have now (almost) finished high school and we have moved out of the province, so the boat has not been used very much for the past few years. We are selling the boat with all the accessories you will need, and a few extras.

The Macgregor 26X is a 'transformer' that can operate as either a sailboat or a motorboat, or a combination of both. There is a port at the stern of the boat that can be opened to allow 1150 pounds of water to flow into a chamber that provides the necessary 'ballast' when you want to sail. A drop keel ensures forward motion and stabilizes the boat and the two rudders ensure you can steer even when the boat is heeled over in the wind. When you need to get home quickly, you can drop the sails and start up the 'iron jib' (a.k.a. the Johnson 50 HP motor). As you move forward on motor power, the water ballast empties and you transform into a motorboat that can reach speeds of over 24 km/hr.

The Macgregor also comes with a trailer and it can be easily pulled behind a car, SUV or truck.
